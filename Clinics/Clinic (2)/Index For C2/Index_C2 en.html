Natural Recipes for Hair Care


Recipes to Promote Hair Growth

1- Strawberry Juice

Strawberries contain Vitamin C, which helps the body absorb iron better and promote hair growth. Just like bananas, strawberries also have small amounts of silica. Vitamin C can help prevent hair breakage and reduce or prevent dandruff formation. Oats are rich in antioxidants and Omega-6 fatty acids, which can strengthen hair follicles, prevent hair loss, and protect hair from damage.

- Ingredients:

  • Half a cup of strawberries
  • 1/4 cup of oats
  • Half a cup of coconut milk
  • 2 dates

- Method:

In a blender, add strawberries, dates, and oats and blend into a smooth paste. Then add the milk and blend until well mixed. Pour the juice into a glass and garnish with almond flakes.


2- Spinach Juice

Spinach is a leafy green plant rich in essential nutrients like folic acid, iron, and vitamins that can help promote hair growth and scalp health, thereby enhancing the growth of new hair follicles.

- Ingredients:

  • Half a cup of bananas or avocado slices
  • Half a cup of spinach leaves
  • Half a cup of coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon matcha powder
  • Sugar or honey to taste

- Method:

In a blender, add bananas, spinach leaves (washed well before use), and sugar or honey to taste and blend into a smooth paste. Then add the milk/almond milk and blend until well mixed.


3- Carrot and Orange Juice

Carrots and oranges are rich in Vitamin C, which helps the body produce collagen, playing an important role in hair building. It can also enhance scalp circulation, stimulating hair follicles for increased growth.

- Ingredients:

  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 cup of coconut water or filtered water
  • 1 orange
  • 1 small piece of ginger
  • Sugar/salt to taste

- Method:

Chop one or two peeled carrots into small pieces and grind them in a blender with a cup of coconut water. You can add sugar or salt. Add the orange pulp and a small piece of ginger, and once the mixture blends into a semi-liquid consistency, you can either strain it or drink it as is.

Recipes to Treat Hair Loss
  • Milk Chocolate with Almonds

    • Milk chocolate with almonds is an essential source of amino acids, which help strengthen and maintain hair health.


  • Soy Protein Drink

    • Soy protein powder contains lysine and Vitamin B-6, which can be mixed with water or with almond or cow's milk to increase the nutrient content for stronger hair.

    Recipes for Hair Nourishment
  • Eggs

    • Eggs are a great source of protein and biotin, essential for producing keratin, a protein for hair growth. Eggs also contain zinc, selenium, and other hair-beneficial nutrients.


  • Berries

    • Berries contain beneficial compounds and vitamins like Vitamin C, which is an antioxidant helping to protect hair follicles from damage and assist in producing collagen for stronger hair.


  • Spinach

    • Spinach is rich in folic acid, iron, and Vitamins A and C, all of which promote hair growth by moisturizing the scalp and enhancing hair strength.


  • Avocado

    • Avocados are rich in Vitamin E, which protects the scalp from oxidative stress. They also provide essential fatty acids that the body doesn't produce.


  • Nuts

    • Nuts are rich in Vitamin B, zinc, and essential fatty acids, all of which contribute to healthy, strong hair.
